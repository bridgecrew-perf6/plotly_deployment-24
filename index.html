Description of the Project

The purpose of this data is to show the types of bacteria which appear in the navels of a selection of Test Subjects. Utilizing the pull down tab, you can select a test subject and see a breakdown of which bacteria were found in their navels, and in what quantity. Additionally, there is a gauge to show how often the subjects cleaned their navels on a weekly basis, as this has a direct impact on the bacteria found.
